Best movie series according to Rafael

A list of my top 3 movie series of all time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (Peter Jackson)

This trilogy has a strong storyline, characters and is packed full of action and drama that made me feel engaged from start to finish.

In order of my own preference:
1) The Return of the King
2) The Fellowship of the Ring
3) The Two Towers

Star Wars Original Trilogy (Eps. IV-VI)

A Sci-Fi film series that revolutionised the application of CGI in cinematography with well-written characters, intra-galactic adventure, plot twists and drama.

In order of my own preference:
1) Episode VI: Return of the Jedi
2) Episode IV: A New Hope
3) Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back

Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) Phases 1-3

The most ambitious movie crossover project ever attempted (and more than successfully executed!), the movies in the MCU bring several heroes and villains of the Marvel comics to life with a strong focus on action, emotional engagement and portrayal of characters in their own unique ways on the big screen.

There are far too many movies for me to even consider listing in order of preference here. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
